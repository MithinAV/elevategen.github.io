Discover our story, mission, and the impact we're making

Our Story

ElevateGen recognizes the pressure students face to have their careers and internships mapped out, even though the steps—applications, gaining experience, and building a path forward—are often unclear. Our mission is to bridge that gap by empowering students to support one another with guidance, resources, and access to opportunities.
